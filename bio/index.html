About Your Therapist

Dr. Alex Morgan, LPC, PhD

Dr. Alex Morgan is a licensed professional counselor with over 10 years of experience helping adults navigate anxiety, depression, life transitions, and relationship challenges.

Alex believes that therapy should be collaborative, compassionate, and grounded in your unique story. Their approach is integrative, drawing from cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and person-centered techniques.

Education & Training

  • Ph.D. in Counseling Psychology – Filler University
  • M.A. in Clinical Mental Health Counseling – Sample State University
  • B.A. in Psychology – Example College
  • Certified in Trauma-Informed Care
  • Ongoing training in mindfulness-based interventions

Therapeutic Approach

In sessions, Alex focuses on creating a space where you feel heard and understood. Together, you’ll explore patterns, identify strengths, and develop practical tools for navigating daily life.

Therapy is not one-size-fits-all; Alex will work with you to tailor the pace, focus, and style to your needs and comfort level.
