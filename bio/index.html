About Your Therapist

Allison Kayyem, MSW

Allison Kayyem is a licensed professional counselor with over 4 years of experience helping adults navigate anxiety, depression, life transitions, and relationship challenges.

Allison believes that therapy should be collaborative, compassionate, and grounded in your unique story. Their approach is integrative, drawing from cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), mindfulness, and person-centered techniques.

Education & Training

  • Masters in Social Work – Colorado State University
  • B.A. in Psychology – Texas State University
  • Certified in Trauma-Informed Care
  • Ongoing training in mindfulness-based interventions

Therapeutic Approach

In sessions, Allison focuses on creating a space where you feel heard and understood. Together, you’ll explore patterns, identify strengths, and develop practical tools for navigating daily life.

Therapy is not one-size-fits-all; Alex will work with you to tailor the pace, focus, and style to your needs and comfort level.
