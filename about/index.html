About Me

My name is Allie Kayyem and I am a Licensed Social Worker (LSW) in Colorado. I work with adults and couples who feel stuck in patterns they can’t quite untangle. Therapy with me is collaborative, grounded, and focused on compassion and clarity.

I draw from CBT, DBT, Trauma-Informed Care, Narrative work, Gottman Method approaches and other strengths-based interventions. Together, we explore what lies beneath the surface and create space for change.
