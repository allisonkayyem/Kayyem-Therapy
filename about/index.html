About Me

When I am off work, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. I appreciate friendly competition, especially board games, that enrich skills of patience, humor, and connection which help guide me in my work. I'm an avid reader and appreciate many different types of books in both the fiction and nonfiction categories. I also value spending time outdoors- whether that is a hike, getting fresh air, or simply being present in nature. I strive to find moments of mindfulness to balance out the hustle of everyday life.
