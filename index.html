Welcome to Kayyem Therapy

My name is Allie Kayyem and I am a Licensed Social Worker. I work with adults and couples who feel stuck in patterns they can’t quite untangle on their own. Therapy with me is collaborative, grounded, and focused on helping you understand yourself with more clarity and compassion. My role is to walk alongside you—helping identify patterns, explore what’s beneath the surface, and create space for meaningful change. I aim to offer a calm, nonjudgmental environment where you don’t have to perform or have it all figured out.

Therapy should be tailored to you—there’s no one-size-fits-all approach. Depending on your needs, I may draw from CBT, DBT, Trauma Informed Care, Gottman Method, Narrative work, and strengths-based interventions. Reaching out can often feel like the hardest step. If you are curious about therapy with me, please feel free to set up a 15 minute consultation to see if working together would be a good fit!

I am a Licensed Social Worker (LSW) in the state of Colorado and practice under clinical supervision as required by state law.
